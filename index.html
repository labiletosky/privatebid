PrivateBid
Blind Auctions RTG • Arcium

Private sealed-bid auctions powered by Arcium.

PrivateBid is a demo for sealed-bid auctions where bids stay hidden until the auction closes. This prevents bid copying, collusion, and early price leakage.

Arcium acts as the privacy layer for encrypted bid handling and private result computation. Only the winning result is revealed after the auction ends.

01. Create Auction

A seller creates an auction for any item or asset.

02. Submit Private Bid

Bidders submit encrypted bids. Other users cannot see the bid amount.

03. Reveal Result

After close, only the winner and final result are revealed.
