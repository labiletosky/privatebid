PrivateBid
Blind Auctions RTG • Solana • Arcium

Private sealed-bid auctions powered by Arcium.

PrivateBid is a Solana-based sealed-bid auction prototype for the Arcium Blind Auctions RTG. It keeps bids private during the auction and reveals only the final result after close.

The goal is simple: fair private price discovery with less bid copying, less collusion, and less MEV leakage.

How Arcium fits

In a normal public auction, every bid can influence other bidders before the auction ends. PrivateBid uses Arcium as the encrypted computation layer so bids remain private while the auction is active.

The private compute flow is: bidder submits encrypted bid → Arcium privacy layer compares bids privately → auction closes → only the winning result is revealed.
